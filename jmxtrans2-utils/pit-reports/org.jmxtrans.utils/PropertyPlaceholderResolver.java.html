/**
28 
 * Inspired by Spring Property placeholder mechanism.
29 
 * <p/>
30 
 * Placeholders are delimited by '{' and '}' and support default value with ':'. Sample : '<code>{graphite.host}</code>'
31 
 * or '<code>{graphite.port:2003}</code>'.
32 
 * <p/>
33

34

35

36

37

38
